Eritrea is a small country in the horn of Africa bordering Ethiopia, Djibouti, and the Red Sea. It is a great place to go scuba diving, pearl fishing, and yacht cruise. Its capital city Asmara has been branded by UN-Habitat as a world heritage site. Eritrea has great marine tourist sites, plenty of flora and fauna, and scenic terrain. This is a place worth visiting to learn more about the hidden treasures of Africa.sing. On uninhabited islands, there are plenty of mangrove trees and salt bushes. There is also a large population of nested birds, coral reefs, and submerged shoals.
